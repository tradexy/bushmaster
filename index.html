Intro

Why have a normal cocktail when you can have a Bushmaster Frozen Cocktail?
Bushmaster Frozen Cocktails, influenced by the American frozen cocktail, born in Britain and blended with a shot of South African charisma. The Bushmaster team are fun loving and dedicated to customer satisfaction. By the way, check out more refreshing photos of awesome cocktails.

We pride ourselves on the quality of our product and our superb service.

Work

Bushmaster Frozen Cocktails delivers unforgettable 1st class frozen cocktails, in a range of flavours that tantalize the taste buds and guarantee to have patrons begging for more. We use quality ingredients so you can be sure to find the perfect summer tipple or whatever the weather. We are satisfied only when our clients are happy.

Bushmaster Frozen Cocktails believes in its duty to the environment, we serve our cocktails in 100% recyclable and re-useable plastic glasses that are easy to carry and fun to keep as a momento of your experience with us. But most of all, kind to Mother Nature!
